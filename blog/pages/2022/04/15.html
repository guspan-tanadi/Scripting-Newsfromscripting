Like many fans of the Grateful Dead I thought Bertha was about a fan in the Dead office named Bertha, but it's the other way around, the fan was given the name of the song. On the other hand, the Beatles song, Martha My Dear is about Paul McCartney's Old English Sheepdog Martha. Speaking of McCartney, I learned recently that he co-wrote the fantastic Elvis Costello song Veronica. It's definitely got the McCartney feel to it, another spin perhaps on Eleanor Rigby, which is not a good song to Peloton to, btw, imho. #
I love all the Beatles songs, but get the most, these days, out of John Lennon's songs. I learned that Lennon only partially completed his songs, leaving a lot of the work to George Martin. Keep that in mind when you listen to I Am The Walrus (my favorite Beatles song) and Strawberry Fields, as examples. The orchestral bits are pure Martin. #
